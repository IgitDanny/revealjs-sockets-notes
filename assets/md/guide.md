### Draggables Areas

<p>Draggables areas helps you navigate
</p>

<u>ActionBar</u>
<p>
Swipe to the top to switch into fullscreen mode
</p>

<u>Swipe Left/Right</u>
<p>
You can swipe to the left to show the plugins and swipe to the right to show the menu.
</p>

<u>Preview Area</u>
<p>
You can navigate in the **preview area** by swiping up / down / left / right
</p>

### Menu

<p>
Menu features : 
<br>

- Toogle the app to fullscreen<br>
- Start or pause the timer<br>
- Reset the timer<br>
- Set the timer (only in minutes)<br>
- Go back to first slide<br>

</p>

<img src="../assets/images/menu.png" width="400px">

### Notes

<p>
If there are note in your presentation they will appear in the **note area**.<br>
<br>
This area is too short ? You could click on arrow to expand the **note area**.
</p>

<pre>
	<img src="../assets/images/notes.png" width="400px">

### Control your presentation

<p>

<br><br>
<u>Step One : </u>  Navigate in the **preview area**<br>
<u>Step Two : </u> Press the button <strong style="color:red">8</strong> to send the preview slide in the remote screen<br>

</p>

<pre>
<img src="../assets/images/remote_howto.png" width="400px">
</pre>

<p>
_Tricks : _<br>

You need to anticipate ? There is a back button in the **preview area**.<br>

_Example : _<br>

<i>Imagine that your are on the slide 2 (in the remote and preview slide is 3). Now, you will navigate in the **preview area** and you want to go back. So you have just to click the "back" button. Your preview will be again on slide 3</i>
</p>

<pre>
<img src="../assets/images/back.png" width="400px">
</pre>
