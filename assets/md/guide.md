### Main Screen

<p>
<strong>Here is how to use the remote</strong>
<br>
<strong>1.</strong> Access to menu<br>
<strong>2.</strong> Access to plugins<br>
<strong>3.</strong> Timer of presentation<br>
<strong>4.</strong> Expand the note to fullScreen mode<br>
<strong>5.</strong> Notes area<br>
<strong>6.</strong> Current slide index<br>
<strong>7.</strong> Preview slide index<br>
<strong>8.</strong> Show the slide in preview Area <br>
<strong>9.</strong> Controls to navigate in presentation (only in preview area)<br>
<strong>10.</strong> Preview Area<br>
</p>

### Draggable Areas

<p>There is some draggable area to help you in your navigation
</p>

<u>ActionBar</u>
<p>
You can move your finger in the actionBar area to the top of the screen to toggle the application in fullscreen mode
</p>

<u>Swype Left/Right</u>
<p>
You can move your finger in the note area to left or right to slide the screen to show the plugins or the menu.
</p>

<u>Preview Area</u>
<p>
You can navigate in the preview area by swyping up / down / left / right
</p>

### Menu

<p>
In the menu you could : 
<br>

- Toogle the app to fullscreen<br>
- Start or pause the timer<br>
- Reset the timer to 0<br>
- Configure the time of timer (only in minutes)<br>
- Force the preview slide to the first slide<br>

</p>

<pre>
<img src="../assets/images/menu.png" width="400px">
</pre>

<br>

### Navigation

<p>
Here how works the navigation: 

<br><br>
<u>First : </u>  You could navigate in the preview area fist buttons <strong style="color:red">9</strong> or by swyping the area <strong style="color:red">10</strong><br>
<u>Second : </u> You could transfert the preview slide in the remote presentation by pressing button <strong style="color:red">8</strong><br>
<u>Third : </u> The number in the <strong style="color:red">6</strong> circle represent the curent slide number (the number of the remote presentation) and the number in the <strong style="color:red">7</strong> circle represent the curent preview slide number.
</p>

<pre>
<img src="../assets/images/remote_howto.png" width="400px">
</pre>

<p>
When you will explore your presentation in the preview area, you may notice that a "back" button appear. This button allow you to return the "+1" slide of the curent slide of remote presentation.<br>
<i>Imagine that your are on the second slide of your presentation (in the remote). Your preview area is on slide 3. Now, you will navigate in the preview area and you want to go back to slide 3. So you have just to click the "back" button.</i>
</p>

<pre>
<img src="../assets/images/back.png" width="400px">
</pre>

## Plugins 


### Play Video

<p>
This plugin allow you to play a video on the curent slide. <br>
You can Mute or unMute the video.<br>
You can play or pause the video.<br>
You can move in the video.<br>
<br>
_For the moment, it only works for html5 video tag_
</p>

<pre>
<img src="../assets/images/plugin-play-video.png" width="400px">
</pre>

### Remote Pointer

<p>
This plugin allow you to draw a circle that will move on the curent slide by following your finger in the preview area. <br>
You change the color of the pointer.<br>
</p>

<pre>
<img src="../assets/images/plugin-remote-pointer.png" width="400px">
</pre>

### Sensor Pointer

<p>
This plugin allow you to draw a circle that will move on the curent slide by following your phone orientation . <br>
You change the color of the pointer.<br>
You have to click on the preview area to activate the plugin and you have to click a second time to stop it.
</p>

<pre>
<img src="../assets/images/plugin-sensor-pointer.png" width="400px">
</pre>
